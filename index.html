How You Can Help

Reduce Plastic Pollution

Millions of tons of plastic enter our oceans each year. Choose reusable items, recycle properly, and participate in local cleanups to make a difference.

Support Sustainable Choices

Make informed decisions about the seafood you eat and the products you buy. Support companies committed to sustainable practices and protecting marine life.

Conserve Marine Habitats

Coral reefs, mangroves, and other coastal habitats are crucial for marine biodiversity. Support organizations working to protect and restore these vital ecosystems.
